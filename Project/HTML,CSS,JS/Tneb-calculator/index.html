Key changes summary
2024 → 2025

All charged slab rates increased by ₹0.15–₹0.35/unit. Free 100 units unchanged. Bills rose ~3–3.5% for most households.

2025 → 2026

Slab rates unchanged. Govt expanded free units from 100 → 200 units for consumers using ≤500 units bi-monthly (effective 10 May 2026).
